About Tanga University Foundation

Our foundation connects current university students and graduates from Tanga and beyond. We aim to foster collaboration, conduct impactful charity work, volunteer in key youth initiatives, and uplift our communities through shared efforts.

  • Youth volunteering and mentorship
  • Organizing charity and donation drives
  • Participating in regional development programs
  • Providing platforms for ideas and unity
