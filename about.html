About Tanga University Foundation

Empowering Scholars | Building Communities | Inspiring Change

Tanga University Foundation is a registered non-profit initiative committed to bringing together university students and alumni from the Tanga region. We aim to build a united platform that promotes academic support, mentorship, and service to the community.

Our work centers on charity outreach, humanitarian programs, educational mentorships, and other student-led projects that address real community challenges. We believe that students are powerful agents of change — capable of solving local issues through collaboration and compassion.

At Tanga Uni Foundation, we promote unity, leadership, and opportunity among youth and scholars — ensuring that no one is left behind. Join us in shaping a brighter future for Tanga and beyond!
